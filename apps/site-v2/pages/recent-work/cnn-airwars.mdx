CNN are a worldwide news network owned by Turner Broadcasting System. They created an interactive article detailing the logistics and operations involved in conducting the war on ISIS from the air.

We worked with design agency c-ll-ct-v-lly to produce a 3D visualisation of airspace and aircraft operating in such a conflict. Contextual video & text content accompanied each altitude, covering reconnaissance, transport, refuelling and communication aspects.
